About

Hi, my name is Adam Stewart! I'm currently in my junior year at Oregon State University (OSU) where I'm pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science with an applied focus on Machine Learning. I've had a passion for the environment and technology for as long as I can remember. Eventually, I hope to use my skill set to aid humanity in the development of sustainable solutions.

I am an active member of the OSU Robotics Club's Mars Rover Team, and right now we are getting ready for the Canadian International Rover Challenge coming up this summer! Additionally, I love getting to share my enthusiasm with the other members of the OSU Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence Club. I also enjoy participating in weekly Capture the Flag competitions in the OSU Security Club.
